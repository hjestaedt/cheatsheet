# terminator

#### __window / tab__

###### __new window__
`shift`+`ctrl`+`i`

###### __new tab__
`shift`+`ctrl`+`t`

###### __close tab / pane__
`ctrl`+`d`

###### __toggle fullscreen__
`F11`

###### __next_tab__
`ctrl`+`page up`

###### __previous_tab__
`ctrl`+`page down`

###### __rename tab__
`ctrl` `,`

#### __pane__

###### __split vertical__
`shift`+`ctrl`+`b`

###### __split horizontal__
`shift`+`ctrl`+`r`

###### __navigate between panes__
`ctrl`+`arrow`

###### __next panes__
`ctrl`+`tab`

###### __previous panes__
`ctrl`+`shift`+`tab`

###### __resize pane__
`shift`+`ctrl`+`arrow`

###### __toggle maximize pane__
`shift`+`ctrl`+`z`

###### __sync panes on__
`shift`+`ctrl`+`a`

###### __sync panes off__
`shift`+`ctrl`+`alt`+`a`

#### __zoom__

###### __zoom in__
`ctrl`+`+`

###### __zoom out__
`ctrl`+`-`

###### __zoom reset__
`ctrl`+`0`
